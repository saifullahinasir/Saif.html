Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I report a crime?
You can report a crime online through our website, call our hotline, or visit your local police station.
2. Is my report confidential?
Yes, all reports are treated confidentially. Your identity will be protected to the fullest extent possible.
3. What types of crimes can I report?
You can report any crime, including theft, assault, vandalism, and drug-related offenses.
4. What information do I need to provide?
Please provide as much detail as possible, including the location, time, and nature of the crime, as well as any suspect information if available.
5. Can I report a crime anonymously?
Yes, you can report a crime anonymously if you prefer. However, providing your contact information can help law enforcement follow up with you.
6. Will I be updated on the status of my report?
Yes, if you provide contact information, you will receive updates regarding the status of your report.
7. What should I do in an emergency?
If you are in immediate danger or need urgent assistance, please call emergency services or your local police department right away.
